About Impact Golf & Fitness

Tracy has been playing the game of golf starting at a young age. She excelled in high school and went on to a successfull collegiate career. Beginning in 2016 Tracy was invited to assist in golf instruction with the professionals at Golf Academy School of Indiana. She studied under her long-time mentor, Erika Wicoff of the LPGA, and began instructing soon after.

Believing that fitness serves major benifits for golfers, Tracy earned her American Council on Exercise (ACE) Personal Trainer Certification. Shortly thereafter Impact Golf & Fitness was born.

My unique service offers multiple ways to improve your life and golf game. Whether you want to tone up, lose a few pounds or improve your golf game, I've got you covered.

Her latest certification came in August 2019, training under famed trainer Shaun T, well known for Insanity.

Tracy is also the Head Girls Golf Coach and Junior Varsity Boys Golf Coach at a local High School.

I really enjoy coaching and making a difference. Let me motivate and make a difference for you. Contact me to schedule a lesson, training session or individual/group fitness classes.
